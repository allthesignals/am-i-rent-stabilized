Step 2 of 5

To find out for sure, request your rent history from the DHCR.

Click here to request it by email.

Or call them at 718 739-6400.

You can also visit a DHCR office.

Be sure to tell them your name, address,  apartment number and that you'd like to  request your rent history.

Step 3 of 5

When you receive your rent history, check it out.

Add a reminder to your calendar.
( It shouldn't take more than a week to arrive in the mail. )

Meanwhile, have your friends and neighbors check their apartment too!

Step 5 of 5

If they were paying a lot less than you are, here's how to take action:

Option 1:

Contact a tenants rights group for advice. These groups can provide useful information and help you take the best course of action.

Option 2:

File a complaint with the DHCR by filling out and mailing this form. But be aware that your landlord will be notified when you file a complaint.
