NYC: am I rent stabilized?

You just may be rent stabilized!

Follow these directions to take action:

  1. Request your rent history from DHCR by emailing them at rentinfo@nyshcr.org or calling them at 718 739-6400.
    Here's a sample email:

    • Hello,
      I, (your name), am currently renting (your address, APARTMENT NUMBER, borough, zipcode) and would like the rent history for the unit I am renting. Any information you can provide me would be greatly appreciated.
      thank you,

      - (your name)

  2. Wait for it in the mail.
  3. When your rent history arrives it will tell you if your apartment is rent stabilized. If your rent is looks way higher than the previous tenant then file a complaint!
